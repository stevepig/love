GUIDE TO MINECRAFT DUNGEONS OUT NOW

Read up and play on!

Many scientific studies (that I may have made up for the sake of this article) say that that is good for your eyes to take a break from your screens. But, the screens are where my video games are! That's why I found this handy “life-hack”: when you can’t play games, read about them. And what better game to read about than the very recently released Minecraft Dungeons! With the Guide to Minecraft Dungeons (released earlier this week alongside the game), you get the best of both worlds.

❮ ❯


This guide is so good for you; it’s a downright educational experience. Really! Want to learn dev insights into what goes into your favorite levels, or a little bit about the gear combos that will totally change your playstyle? Guide to Minecraft Dungeons contains 96 information-packed pages of all the things you’ve ever wanted to know! That is if everything you wanted to know was all about Minecraft Dungeons.

Okay, that’s all you’re going to get out of me – if you want to find out more, you’re going to have to buy the book yourselves!
